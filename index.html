introduction

Xue Yuerong is currently pursuing the M.S in Control Engineering with the Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen. She is mentored by Prof. Jun Xu and Prof. Fanglin Chen in School of Computer Science and Technology, HITSZ. Her current research interests include piecewise linear neural network, representation learning, generative model, Bayesian network as well as their application in multimedia.

She received the B.Eng degree of Electrical Engineering from Xiamen University in July 2020.
